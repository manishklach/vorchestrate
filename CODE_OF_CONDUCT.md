# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, technically serious, and collaborative community.

## Expected Behavior

- engage with curiosity and professionalism
- critique ideas and code, not people
- keep feedback specific, evidence-based, and constructive
- share benchmarks, traces, and claims responsibly

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- intentionally misleading technical claims
- hostile review behavior that makes collaboration harder

## Reporting

If you experience or witness problematic behavior, contact `manishklach@gmail.com`.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, remove content, or restrict participation when needed to preserve a healthy working environment.
